Description

Our company New Tech, offers tutoring to students who are having difficulty with a subject or wish to understand better a particular area about Computer Science. It can also be helpful for students who wish to learn more information and acquire new abilities in order to pursue a profession in computer science.

Depending on the instructor and student preferences, tutoring sessions may be done online or in person. To assist the student in learning and comprehending the subject matter, the tutor may use a variety of teaching techniques during the sessions, including lectures, demonstrations, discussions, and exercises.

The tutor may work on certain subjects such programming languages, data structures, algorithms, software development processes, or computer hardware. Also, they could help the student finishing tasks, assignments and for exam preparation.
